Earnest Shmelingstein - Botanist/ Agriculture/Experience in cloning/ Chef/Cook

Earnest was born in Split, Croatia on Jul. 15, 1973. He grew up in a family of 10 and his parents were farmers. He is an expert in Agriculture, studying at the University of Split’s Faculty of Agriculture. He has a PhD in Agriculture from there. He also has a Master’s degree in Animal Science from McGill University, studying cloning of animals and plants there. He has a hobby of cooking and loves it, whether he’s cooking for family or friends. He is married to Ernita Shmelingstein and they have 5 kids: Milka, Sofija, Tena, Nemanja, and Joe. He works for BioFusion Tech as a cloner. Earnest has been appointed as the head of the Mars Cloning Department or MCD and plans to be in charge of cultivating the development of animal and plant life on Mars.
