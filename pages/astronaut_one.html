Quibuleous Shiboinker - Engineer Degree/Astronaut/Nurse Degree/Architecture/ Surveyor/ Robotics

Mr. Shiboinker is a world renowned Engineer, Astronaut, Nurse, Architect, Surveyor, as well as a Robotics expert with degrees in robotics. His claim to fame was when he co-founded Macrosoft alongside Bull Gates. Quibuleous was born in Hotazel, South Africa June 15th 1986 to mother Sheeva Shiboinker and father Quibulord Shiboinker and is an only child. His parents were gem miners and Quibuleous grew up in poverty but then immigrated to Houlton, Maine when he was 12 years old. Quibuleous attended Harvard, Yale, as well as University of South Africa, Cape Town, and has honorary degrees from UC Irvine, UCLA and Princeton. Quibuleous is taking his talents to Mars leaving behind his legacy (Net worth 36 billion) to his wife Theedleton, son Smeegleton, and daughters Moochaton and Choochaton precluding a safe return. Quibuleous is in charge of the surveying department of the mars mission and is set to be the first man to fully survey Mars topography as well as to name and rename rediscovered and newly found landmarks.
